Alfredo Chicken Pasta


Meet the chicken pasta recipe of your dreams! Creamy Chicken Alfredo with fried chicken garlic and cheese.There comes your quick 20 minute dinner!
Ingredients
  • Sliced chicken- 200g
  • Garlic- 4
  • Kasmiri chilli powder- 1 tbs
  • Ginger - a small piece
  • Garlic powder- 2 tsp
  • Onion powder- 1 tsp
  • Salt- as required
  • Soya sauce- 1 tsp
  • Egg- 1
  • Oil- as required
  • Milk- 300 ml
  • All purpose flour- 1 tbs
  • Butter- as required
  • Cheese cubes- as required
  • Pasta- 300g
  • Oregano- a pinch

Preparation
Marinate chicken with ginger garlic paste,kashmiri chilli powder,garlic powder, onion powder,soya sauce,egg and salt. Keep aside for atleast 15 minutes. After 1 hour fry the chicken in oil. While that time boil water and add pasta with some salt and oil.
Now start preparing the alfredo pasta, take a pan and add some butter, add flour and saute for a minute. Then add some milk and cheese , mix well until you get a required consistency, then add the fried chicken, saute for a minute, finally add required salt, peper and oregano.
Its ready!.
